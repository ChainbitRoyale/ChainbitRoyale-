The Problem

Why Traditional Gaming Fails Players.

Players invest countless hours and financial resources into in-game items, yet possess **no real ownership**. These assets are locked to centralized accounts, cannot be freely traded, and hold no value outside the game‚Äôs ecosystem. This model benefits the platform, not the player.

The Solution

True Asset Ownership.

Chainbit Royale uses blockchain to transform every cosmetic item, skin, and outfit into verifiable **NFTs**. This gives you full, uncensored control over your digital assets, allowing you to **own, trade, and monetize** them freely in our player-owned economy.

Core Mechanics

Play-to-Earn Tournaments in Fracture

Variable Stakes

Enter tournaments by paying a fee in **CBR** token. We offer **different stake games to accommodate every player‚Äôs investment level** from casual matches to high-stakes contests.

Self-Sustaining Economy

A **minimal percentage** of the entry fee pool is automatically deducted for team funding and operational costs, ensuring the long-term health of the game.

Instant Payout

The **overwhelming majority** of the collected fee pool is instantly and verifiably distributed to the tournament winner(s) via smart contract execution.
