The Problem

Why Traditional Gaming Fails Players.

Players invest countless hours and financial resources into in-game items, yet possess **no real ownership**. These assets are locked to centralized accounts, cannot be freely traded, and hold no value outside the game’s ecosystem. This model benefits the platform, not the player.

Discover the Chainbit Solution

The Solution

True Asset Ownership.

Chainbit Royale uses blockchain to transform every cosmetic item, skin, and outfit into a verifiable **NFTs**. This gives you full, uncensored control over your digital assets, allowing you to **own, trade, and monetize** them freely in our player-owned economy.

Core Mechanics

Play-to-Earn Tournaments in Fracture

Variable Stakes

Enter tournaments by paying a fee in **CBR** token. We offer **different stake games to accommodate every player’s investment level**, from casual matches to high-stakes contests.

Self-Sustaining Economy

A **minimal percentage** of the entry fee pool is automatically deducted for team funding and operational costs, ensuring the long-term health of the game.

Instant Payout

The **overwhelming majority** of the collected fee pool is instantly and verifiably distributed to the tournament winner(s) via smart contract execution.

Temporary Transaction Tax

A **3.00% tax** is currently applied to all **buy and sell transactions** on Decentralized Exchanges (DEXs) until the game's official release.

  • Purpose: Funding initial development, marketing, and audits.
  • Wallets/P2P: Transfers between standard wallets are **tax-free**.
Post-Launch Policy: Once Chainbit Royale officially launches (Phase 4), the transaction tax will be set to **0%** to encourage full utility.
